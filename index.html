Arun Venkatraman

PhD Robotics, Engineer at Aurora Innnovation working on self-driving vehicles.

I was a PhD student at the Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University working with Dr. J. Andrew (Drew) Bagnell and Dr. Martial Hebert, focusing on the intersection of machine learning and robotics. I have also often collaborated with Dr. Byron Boots from the Georgia Institute of Technology.

See my Google Scholar or CMU Robotics Institute page for a more complete publication list.
